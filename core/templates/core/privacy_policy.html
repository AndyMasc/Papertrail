Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 4, 2026

At Papertrail, we believe your financial records are your private property. We have built this platform with a "privacy-first" architecture, meaning we minimize what we know about your files and maximize your control over them.

1. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: We exclusively support secure, passwordless authentication and social logins via Google and GitHub. We only collect basic profile details (email and name) to manage your account.
  • Usage Data: We use minimal, non-identifying cookies strictly for managing your local timezone settings to ensure your records are accurate.
  • Technical Logs: Basic server logs to maintain the security and integrity of our systems.
  • Document Metadata: We store metadata (filename, timestamp) to help you organize and retrieve your records.

2. How We Handle Your Files & AI Processing

We use Cloudflare R2 for secure storage and the Gemini API for intelligent organization. Our security standards include:

  • Direct, Secure Uploads: Files are uploaded directly from your device to storage using time-limited, signed URLs (30-minute lifespan).
  • AI-Powered Insights: We transmit specific document content to the Gemini API solely to extract information (dates, amounts, categories) to populate your records.
  • Zero-Retention Policy: Our Gemini API integration is configured to not retain your document data or use it to train Google's machine learning models.
  • Private Ownership: Your files and extracted data are linked exclusively to your unique user ID.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. Data is shared only if required by law or with our core infrastructure partners (Cloudflare, Google/Gemini, and our hosting provider) to deliver our services.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Retention: We retain your account information and document metadata as long as your account remains active.
  • Permanent Deletion: You have the right to delete your records at any time. When triggered, your files are permanently scrubbed from our Cloudflare R2 storage and our primary database.
  • Archives: Archives serve as a "soft delete" feature, allowing you to restore records while helping us maintain accurate, clean statistics for your dashboard.

5. Your Rights

  • Right to be Forgotten: Upon closing your account, all associated personal data, metadata, and uploaded files are permanently and securely deleted from our systems.

6. Contact Us

If you have questions about how we handle your data, reach out to our privacy team at: support@papertrail.app
