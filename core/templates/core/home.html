Frequently Asked Questions.

What is Papertrail?

Papertrail helps you organize receipts, warranties, and important purchase records in one place.

How does receipt scanning work?

Upload a photo and Papertrail automatically extracts information. You can review and edit the details manually if needed.

Is my data secure?

Your receipts and documents are stored securely and are only accessible from your account.

Does Papertrail make digital reciepts?

Yes. Papertrail digitally recreates your reciepts, so you can use them all from your phone.
